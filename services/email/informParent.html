About Us

spikeview is a global, fully secure platform that enables kids to narrate their life stories, experiences and achievements and then customize the views of their stories with as much or as little information they wish to share. The platform also provides a unique ability to build and engage with their global network, capture recommendations and tap into tailored opportunities that align with their interests and aspirations. The data is fully secure, owned and controlled only by the user eliminating all bad actors. spikeview is the fastest growing global platform among teens who want a private space to catalog their experiences that make them unique and leverag that to seek globally differentiating opportunities
